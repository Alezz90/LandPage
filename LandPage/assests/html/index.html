The village is built on the side ofa mountain , with stone houses and narrow alleyways that wind through the village. The architecture is a blend of traditional Omani style and the natural landscape, creating a picturesque setting. The village is also famous for its ancient falaj system, an ingenious irrigation system that brings water from nearby springs to the terraced gardens.
